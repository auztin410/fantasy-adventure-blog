Entry One

October 29, 2024

Hm... another night camping within these tunnels, deep within the Earth... The sounds of nothing, days worth of stone in every direction, it can make a man mad you know?

These old tunnels aren't as bad as they say, at least they ain't as bad as facing the lawbringer, Ha! Just have to keep out of sight for a few days, is all.

The worst part was just waiting for my eyes to adjust to the utter darkness down here, It isn't perfect, but I don't have enough fuel for torches down here.

Wouldn't want to draw attention to me, with such a thing anyway, no need to take note of me, I am quiet as the dead and will be gone before long, take no notice of me.
