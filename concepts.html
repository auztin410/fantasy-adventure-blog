Sundown Protocol

April 12th, 2025

After the last satellite fails, humanity must relearn how to communicate across vast, darkened continents.

This concept explores a post-digital future where all high-bandwidth systems have collapsed. It focuses on a wandering archivist who takes it upon himself to traverse the lands full of dangers from the environment along with the locals where electricity is the currency of choice in the form of batteries, using relics of the past that little is known about.

    Themes: Sci-fi Entropy Memory Obsolete Tech
    Format: Short Story Story
    Inspiration: Kenshi Book of Eli Fallout
