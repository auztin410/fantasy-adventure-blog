They Are Lying To You

April 6th, 2025

What if reality was nothing like what your perception of it?

Reality isn’t what you think…

Your perception, no! The construction of this reality of yours and the vast majority is a lie.

Every single piece has been planted and set in place for a purpose.

And I mean every piece.


You have constructed what the world is like, without ever really seeing it, of events, and what people are like that you have never met.


It is all fabricated a lie stacked upon a lie.


The layers of these lies run deep and some have transcended lies themselves…

What do I mean by that? When is a lie no longer required?

When no one questions a thing anymore.

Working for someone else, working after hours, when you do what you believe, what you were

made to believe is right, you no longer need to lie.


You believe it as fact, some require more lies and others less, that vacation or retirement perhaps, a fabrication that you will never see.


While others require lies of what reality is.

Requiring the knowledge that good triumphs over evil.

That there is more good in this world than bad.


Now what if I told you that was all made up?

Set up to keep you where you are right now.


You likely wouldn’t care.

You no longer need the lies.

You accept because it is what you were made to do.


I cannot free your mind of this, I cannot shine a light upon the truth.


Not because I have no light or truth, but because you would simply…


Squint and turn away. So adverse to the light, such a foreign thing it is to you.
